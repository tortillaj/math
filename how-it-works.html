How It Works

Students apply their developmental math skills to solve real world challenges.

Here’s how it works:

  1. The instructor chooses modules based on their course needs.
  2. Students log-in to access the modules selected by their instructor.
  3. Each module features a series of animated “real world” scenarios followed by a math problem.
  4. Once the math problem is answered correctly, the student moves on to the next scenario. If answered incorrectly, we guide them through the answer (step-by-step). Then we ask them a series of similar problems to ensure they have learned the material, before advancing to the next scenario.
  5. A new module cannot be started until the student completes their current module with a passing score (scoring metric is determined by the instructor).
  6. A student roster (showing their grades and progress) is always available.
